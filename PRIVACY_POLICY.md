## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Personal Information

When you create an account, we collect:

- **Email address** - For account creation and authentication
- **Display name** - For personalization (optional)
- **Password** - Stored securely using industry-standard hashing (we never see your plain-text password)

### 1.2 User-Generated Content

When you use the App, we store:

- **Sermons** - AI-generated sermons you create
- **Bookmarks** - Bible verses you bookmark
- **Highlights** - Bible verses you highlight with colors
- **Notes** - Personal notes you add to verses
- **Search history** - Bible searches you perform (stored locally only)

### 1.3 Subscription Information

If you purchase a subscription, we collect:

- **Subscription tier** - Free, Basic, Premium, or Ministry
- **Purchase receipts** - From Google Play for verification
- **Payment method** - Processed by Google Play (we never see your credit card details)

### 1.4 Usage Analytics

We collect anonymous usage data to improve the App:

- **App usage patterns** - Which features you use, how often
- **Error reports** - Crash logs and error messages
- **Device information** - Device model, OS version, app version
- **Performance metrics** - Load times, response times

**Note**: Analytics data is anonymized and cannot be linked to your personal identity.

### 1.5 Information We DO NOT Collect

We do **NOT** collect:

- ‚ùå Location data
- ‚ùå Contacts or phonebook
- ‚ùå Camera or microphone access (unless you explicitly grant for future features)
- ‚ùå SMS or call logs
- ‚ùå Other apps on your device

## 4. Data Security

### 4.1 Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

- **Encryption in Transit** - All data transmitted over HTTPS/TLS
- **Encryption at Rest** - Database encrypted by Supabase
- **Password Hashing** - Passwords hashed with bcrypt (industry standard)
- **Authentication** - JWT tokens with expiration
- **Database Security** - Row-Level Security (RLS) policies ensure users can only access their own data

### 4.2 Your Responsibility

You are responsible for:

- Keeping your password secure and confidential
- Not sharing your account with others
- Logging out on shared devices
- Reporting security issues to us immediately

### 4.3 Data Breach Notification

In the unlikely event of a data breach, we will:

1. Investigate the breach immediately
2. Notify affected users within 72 hours
3. Provide guidance on protecting your account
4. Report to relevant authorities as required by law

## 5. Data Retention

### 5.1 Active Accounts

We retain your data **as long as your account is active** to provide the service.

### 5.2 Account Deletion

When you delete your account:

1. Your personal information (email, display name) is **deleted within 30 days**
2. Your user-generated content (sermons, notes) is **permanently deleted**
3. Anonymous analytics data may be retained for statistical purposes
4. Subscription history is retained for legal/financial compliance (up to 7 years as required by law)

### 5.3 Inactive Accounts

If you haven't logged in for **2 years**, we may:

- Send you a reminder email
- Archive your account (can be restored on login)
- Delete your account after 3 years of inactivity (with prior notice)
