## TROUBLESHOOTING

### Problem: Page won't load

**Solution**:
1. Make sure you typed `localhost:8081` correctly
2. Try `http://localhost:8081` with http://
3. Refresh page (Ctrl+R or F5)

### Problem: White screen or errors

**Solution**:
1. Press F12 to open browser console
2. Look for errors
3. Try different browser (Chrome, Edge, Firefox)
4. Clear browser cache (Ctrl+Shift+Delete)

### Problem: Can't see Bible verses

**Solution**:
- The app currently has only John chapter 3
- Go to John → Chapter 3 to see verses
- Full Bible will be added later
